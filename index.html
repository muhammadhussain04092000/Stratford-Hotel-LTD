About Us

Unveiling our story

Stratford Hotel LTD is situated in the heart of Stratford in London's East end. Our address is 43 Romford Road, London, E15 4LY. The hotel has a total of 22 rooms, each with en-suite bathroom which contains a shower, toilet and wash basin. Each room is equipped with a TV set, Tea and Coffee making facilities and free Wi-Fi internet access.
